Who We Are

Code the Block is a Kansas City based non-profit that teaches the fundamentals of software development to disconnected youth to help them prepare for careers in software development. Most of our students are low-income, in high school or recently graduated.

Mission

To accelerate growth of the tech and startup community through comprehensive educational programs.
